Dimsum can be translated to mean snack. In this experience we will be trying out iconic dimsum as well as some other snacks beloved by the locals. We then make our way to a hole-in-the-wall dumpling place and try out some fresh hand made dumplings. Soon after, we'll squeeze our way through the long lines and try out some dimsum classics such as Crystal Shrimp Dumplings and Beancurd Rolls washed down with iced milk tea. We'll continue our walk deeper into Chinatown and try a different kind of bun, filled with a meatball. Then top off our experience with sweet treats from 2 different bakeries.
