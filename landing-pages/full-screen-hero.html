Frequently asked questions

Who will manage my property in Antibes?

Luckey will manage your property, with the help of our partners and their team who specialize in mid-term and short-term rental management in Antibes. They will oversee the on-the-ground operations (cleaning, check-in etc.), while Luckey will handle the online operations (listing creation, booking requests etc.) and the guest relationship. You will get an opportunity to meet our partners or a representative from their team before using the service.

Am I covered if something goes wrong?

Whenever a guest makes a reservation and stays at your place, you’re automatically covered by our Host Guarantee, which provides property damage coverage of up to $1,000,000 USD. If a claim needs to be filed, your hosting team can help you take care of it. Learn more about Airbnb’s Host Guarantee

Am I eligible for hosting services?

You are eligible to use the service if you have an entire home that you’d like to list, whether it’s a vacation rental or city apartment, located in one of the cities where Luckey has partnered with an expert team – and if your property matches their local criteria.

Are my personal belongings safe?

You don’t need to empty your space in order to host – Airbnb makes sure travelers know that they are staying in someone’s home. You may want to store valuables in a secure place, such as a locked room, closet, safe, or storage facility. Ask your team to help you figure out the best solution for your belongings.

Can I offer property management services through Airbnb?

Finding new and better ways to help our host community is a top priority, and we have partnered with a handful of hosting teams to learn more about how we can make hosting easier. As this is currently a closed pilot, we are not accepting applications for partnerships at this time.

When do I collect the earnings?

After each reservation, an amount corresponding to 80% of the revenue generated will be credited to your account directly.

Does Luckey also manage mid- and long-term stays?

Whatever your plan is for hosting your property, Luckey is here to support you! Our experts can advise you on how to boost your occupancy and maximize your income based on the time of year and where your property is located. Our services are suitable for guest stays of any length. From setting the right rate, ensuring optimal availability, providing guests with thoughtful amenities, and even preparing a rental contract when needed - we’ll be by your side every step of the way.
